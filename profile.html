About Me

Software Engineering Student!

I am a student at the African Leadership University, originally from Ghana but currently residing in Nairobi. I am passionate about contributing to the growth and development of Africa, particularly by bridging the digital skills gap and creating opportunities for young people on the continent.

As a woman in STEM, I am proud to be part of a community that is committed to driving change and innovation. I am an active contributor to an open-source program called GirlScript, where I collaborate with others to develop projects that promote gender equality in technology.

Furthermore, I am currently enrolled in the ALX Data Science course, where I am expanding my knowledge and skills in this rapidly evolving field.
